# Overcast Color Palette
**EXACT Raycast Overcast Theme Colors**

A soft, professional light theme with sky blue background and deep blue text.

## Base Colors

### Backgrounds
- `bg_primary`:     `#D8E5EB` - Main background (light sky blue)
- `bg_secondary`:   `#C5D8ED` - Selected/emphasized background
- `bg_tertiary`:    `#E0EAF0` - Hover states
- `bg_dark`:        `#B8CFDD` - Darker elements (borders)

- `fg_primary`:     `#0D296D` - Primary text (deep blue)

## Usage Notes

1. This is a **light theme** - use dark text on light backgrounds
2. Maintain sufficient contrast (WCAG AA minimum: 4.5:1 for normal text)
3. Use muted, desaturated colors to reduce eye strain
4. Purple and blue are primary accent colors
5. Keep neon/bright colors minimal compared to dark themes
